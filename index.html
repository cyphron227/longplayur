Spotify no longer opens its API to independent apps, so Longplayur can't offer a normal log-in. Instead you create your own free Spotify app. Five minutes, once. This page then runs entirely in your browser using it, and nothing about your listening ever leaves your machine.

  1. 01

    Copy the redirect URI

    You will paste this into your Spotify app's settings in step 03.

  2. 02

    Create a Spotify app

    This step needs a computer: the Spotify developer dashboard is not built for small screens.

    Send this page to your computer:

    Open the Spotify developer dashboard and create an app. Any name and description will do.

  3. 03

    Add the redirect URI to your app

    In your new app's settings, paste the redirect URI you copied in step 01. It must match exactly.

  4. 04

    Paste your client ID

    Find it on your app's dashboard page.

Spotify requires a Premium account for this.
